Session One - Laconic

The gift giving industry is becoming increasingly commoditized, taking away many of the special aspects that gifting should allow for. At BP, we believe that society is ready for a change in the social conventions about how we think about gift-giving, and what the process should look like. We understand that some people like to be surprised, while others may want a particular item. Through Laconic, we are introducing a mechanism in order to make the gift giving process as seamless and transparent as possible for all parties involved.
